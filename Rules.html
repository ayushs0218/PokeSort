Pokémon TCG Rules
~ Pokémon TCG Rules ~
  1. Deck Must Include 60 Cards
  2. Deck Must Contain Energy Cards Sufficient Enough To Attack
  3. In The Initial Turn Of Both Players, There Must Be An Active Basic Pokémon And An Optional Benched Pokémon
  4. In Order To Play A Pokémon Of An Upper Evolution, That Pokémon Must Be Evolved To With Previous Evolutions
  5. Top 6 Cards Of The Shuffled Deck Goes To The "Prize Card" Pile
  6. Players Are Allowed To Put Basic Pokémon Cards Immediately From Their Hand To the Bench
  7. Players May Evolve As Many Pokémon As They Want In Their Turn.
  8. Players May Only Attach 1 Energy Card To 1 Pokémon Card, Once Per Turn
  9. Players May Play As Many Trainer Cards, But Only One Supporter Card Per Turn
  10. Players Are Only Allowed To Retreat 1 Pokémon Per Turn
  11. Players May Use As Many Abilities As They Wish Per Turn
